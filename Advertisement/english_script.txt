During this auspicious Vesak and Poson season, we invite you to receive merit through your generous support.
 
The Dhamma Sarana Vihara in Keysborough conducts daily religious and spiritual activities that require significant resources. We kindly request your generous contribution to help sustain this sacred institution.

You or your family can dedicate a donation in honor of a special day for your loved ones. The daily expense amounts to two hundred and eighty dollars, which can be fully sponsored for a day of your choosing. You may also set up a monthly direct debit of twenty-five dollars.
 
We also warmly encourage families to join together — with the support of one hundred and fifty families, we can sustain this mission as a community.
 
For donation details, contact information, and bank account details, please refer to the information displayed on screen.
